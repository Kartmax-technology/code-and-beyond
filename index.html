What is Code & Beyond?

Code & Beyond is our internal knowledge-sharing platform where team members present on technical topics, share insights, and foster collaboration across departments.

  • ✓ Weekly sessions every Wednesday from 5-6 PM
  • ✓ 45-60 minute format with dedicated Q&A time
  • ✓ Diverse topics from technical skills to soft skills
  • ✓ Open platform for all team members to contribute
